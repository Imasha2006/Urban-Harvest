Our Mission: Sustainable Living for All

Supporting local farmers, reducing food waste, and building a greener future together.

Who We Are

Founded in 2020, Urban Harvest connects conscious consumers with local organic farmers

Urban Harvest was born from a simple idea: what if we could make sustainable living easier and more accessible? We noticed that many people wanted to support local farmers and reduce their environmental impact, but found it challenging to source quality, eco-friendly products consistently.

Today, we partner with over 100 local organic farmers within a 50-mile radius, delivering fresh, seasonal produce and zero-waste products to thousands of households. Every box we deliver represents a step toward a more sustainable future.
